The App That Is Changing The Music Game.

The best music in the universe

SpaceTunes is a simple music app for music lovers. It has two main ways of listening to music. First the basic mode, this is your normal but not typical form of listening to your favorite music. The basic mode is similar to other music apps. Then the second mode, or the Hipster mode. This mode only features songs and artist that are not big yet, but are waiting to break out. You select the genre you would like to hear. You listen to the undiscovered songs, and vote whether you like the song or not. This process leads to our "Top Undiscovered" playlist where artist can be noticed by the public for their great music. Not only does it help you find new great music, but it helps the artist reach new levels of exposure. Sounds great right? Go ahead, download it for your mobile device or give it a try in our browser app. You will love it!
